The Premise

The algorithm has cooked us—our attention spans fried, our choices made for us, our time spent scrolling instead of reading. This is the antidote. A monthly book subscription where you surrender the choice. You tell us your mood. We hunt down one book from the dusty stacks of India's oldest markets and send it to your door. No recommendations engine. No infinite scroll. Just paper, and the quiet act of reading.
